During the summer, I worked on a comprehensive project using Quantum ESPRESSO and VESTA for first-principles simulations. I constructed crystal structures, created alloys and supercells, and calculated properties like bulk modulus, band structure, and density of states for materials such as Silicon and Nickel.

  • Crystal symmetry analysis & electronic property calculations
